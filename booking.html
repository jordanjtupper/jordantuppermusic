notes from the road

Jordan's background spans music education, performance, and program leadership — nearly two decades of making sure rehearsals, sets, and events run on time and on point. Whether it's a wedding reception, a corporate function, or a weekend residency, that means clear communication before the date and a professional, dependable presence on it.

Each project brings its own flavor: Night Hog for high-energy classic rock and funk, Cover 6 Band for a broad crowd-pleasing set list suited to weddings and private events, and Jim Standley Jazz for a more intimate, standards-driven evening.
